The Problem:

This problem is really twofold: First, the Emerald Media Group is an independent news group on campus that has produced the school newspaper for over 100 years. As print readership declined the organization needed new revenue streams to support the paper as it transitioned to a digital first platform. Second, many local businesses in Eugene rely on the unversity student demographic in order to stay viable, but many owners and marketing professionals struggle to stay on top of trends and technolgy.

Client: UO Architecture and Allied Arts

The University of Oregon School of Architecture and Allied Arts was founded in 1914 and is a national leader in architecture, the arts, planning, policy, and design. The school celebrated its 100th anniversary in 2014 and asked the Venture Dept. to create a video that embodied the rich history while also celebrating the future. Two of the most tallented people I know made this possible: Grant Lemons shot, edited and animated, and Monica Sagowitz wrote the script.
