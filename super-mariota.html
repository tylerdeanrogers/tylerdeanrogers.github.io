Super Mariota

A new iOS game for the true fans

The Problem:

Marcus Mariota is the best football player to ever play at the Unversity of Oregon. As Duck fans we needed a way to show our appreciation for our beloved QB.

The Solution:

An 8-bit iOS game right in time for the 2015 NFL Draft.Cody Hatfield and Jeremy Klein built the app and the made audio tracks. I was responsible for all the Illustrator work and character design. It was my first time making pixel art, but the process was pretty simple. We've submitted the game to the App Store and now we're hoping it's accepted before the Draft. I'll keep this updated. Also, check out the landing page I threw together and don't forget to download the app.
