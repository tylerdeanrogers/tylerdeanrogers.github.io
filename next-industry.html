Next Industry

Exploring the future of the web design industry

The Problem:

As soon to be college graduates in the creative industry we wanted to gain more insight into the world of web design. It’s daunting to try and plan a career when the platform you’re attempting to specialize in is changing so fast, so we wanted to find out what’s next. Is there a long term career for web designers? What new technologies can the world expect to see in the next few years?

The Solution:

We reached out to some of the most tallented individuals in the industry (Paul Irish, Jon Wiley, Leigh Taylor just to name drop a few) through twitter and asked them a simple question: “What’s next for the web design industry?” We paired our own research with the insight we gained from Twitter and made a video and microsite that displayed our results. I had a blast working with Grant Lemons and Marino Lapidus. This project actually had a huge impact on me.
